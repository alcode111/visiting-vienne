Visit Vienne

Discover history here in Vienne with all its ancient monuments dating back to the times of the Roman Empire.

Top 3 sites

St-Maurice Cathedral

There is a mention of the cathedral in the beginning of the 4th century. Some memorable events happened here such as the Council of Vienne in 1311 to decide the fate of the Knights Templar.

Temple of Augustus and Livia

It was initially a temple dedicated to the imperial cult, to honor the Emperor Augustus and and his wife Livia. In more recent times it was turned into a parish churched before it regained its original temple form.

Antic Theater

Coupled with an odeon and dating back as far as the 1st century, it is yet another architectural building charged with history. One of the few odeons in the world that has the monumental latin inscription of "odeum".
